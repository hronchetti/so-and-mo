Why we started

In our experience, we found that domestic lawn care products in general are made to be complex, give disappointing results and force customers into a decision they’re not trained for.

We recognised the desire for a bright and healthy lawn was outweighed by the chore that came with achieving it.

So we decided to do something about it.
